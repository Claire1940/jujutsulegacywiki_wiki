<Section icon={Target} title="How to Unlock Traits: Raid Progression Guide">

Unlocking the best **Jujutsu Legacy traits** requires a systematic approach to the game's progression. You cannot jump straight into the Divine General Raid. You must follow a specific leveling route to ensure your character has the health and damage output to survive the endgame bosses.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Level 1-500 Foundation">
    Focus on the Shibuya and Metro progression routes. Defeat bosses like **Choso** and **Mahito** to stabilize your Level. During this phase, use your free Technique Spins to land an A-Tier technique like **Cursed Manipulation** or **Disaster Flames**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Culling Games & Vessel Acquisition">
    Move into the Culling Games area (Level 500-700). Farm **Uro** and **Ishigori** for their respective Vessel items. Having a Vessel like **Sukuna** or **Ishigori** is a prerequisite for high-tier trait synergy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Raid Preparation (Level 1000+)">
    Reach the Shinjuku route and begin farming late-game bosses like **Kashimo**. You should have a stable V2 technique (e.g., **Ten Shadows V2**) and high-end accessories like the **Inverted Spear of Heaven** before attempting raids.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Farming the Strongest Raid">
    This raid is the source of the **Six Eyes** and **Honored One** traits. Teamwork is essential here; ensure you have a dedicated "tank" and high-DPS sorcerers to clear the waves within the time limit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Divine General Raid">
    The ultimate challenge for the **King of Curses** and **Heian Emperor** traits. This raid requires a perfect build and knowledge of Sukuna's attack patterns. Success here grants the most powerful traits in the 2026 meta.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Raid Reward Tip">
Raids don't just drop traits; they also drop high-tier accessories like the **Sukuna Heian Haori**. Even if you don't get the trait you want, the equipment drops will significantly boost your overall power level.
</Callout>
