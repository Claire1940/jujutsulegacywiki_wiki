<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Accessory Farming Guide">

Farming top-tier accessories in Jujutsu Legacy requires preparation, patience, and strategy. Bosses have low drop rates, meaning you must optimize your farming runs to maximize efficiency. Follow this structured approach to secure your endgame gear.

| Boss Name | Level Req | Location | Drop Rate |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Gojo | 150+ | Jujutsu High | 1% Drop |
| Sukuna | 150+ | Cursed Valley | 2% Drop |
| Toji | 120+ | Shibuya Alley | 1.5% Drop |
| Yuta | 100+ | Sendai Colony | 5% Drop |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Level and Gear Check">
    Ensure your character is at least Level 120 before attempting to farm Special Grade bosses. Equip B-tier or A-tier gear like Yuta's Ring to ensure you have enough health and cursed energy to survive the encounters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Boss Mechanics Mastery">
    Study boss attack patterns. Bosses like Gojo use area-of-effect (AoE) attacks that can defeat unprepared players in one hit. Master your dash timing and defensive techniques to minimize damage taken.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Cooperative Farming">
    Join public servers or farm with a party. Defeating bosses in groups increases the speed of each run, allowing you to complete more attempts per hour and significantly improving your chances of obtaining rare drops.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Stat Optimization">
    Once you acquire your desired accessory, visit the Refinement NPC to roll for optimal secondary stats. Focus on stats that complement your build, such as Cooldown Reduction for cursed users or Critical Chance for physical fighters.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Drop Rate Caution">
Drop rates in Jujutsu Legacy are subject to RNG. Be prepared to defeat bosses dozens of times before seeing an S-Tier accessory drop. Using drop rate boosts during server events is highly recommended.
</Callout>
