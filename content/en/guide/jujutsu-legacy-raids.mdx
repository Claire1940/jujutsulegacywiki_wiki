<Section icon={Swords} title="Culling Games Raid Strategy and Scaling">

The Culling Games raid is the perfect training ground for newer players. It introduces the wave-based survival format and gives a clear picture of how enemy health pools scale as the raid progresses. While early waves feature basic enemies with minimal health, the difficulty spikes significantly every ten waves when bosses spawn.

At Wave 10, players will face the first major boss, Jogo, who possesses approximately 85,000 HP. While this may seem daunting to beginners, Jogo's attacks are highly telegraphed and easy to dodge. By the time you reach Wave 50, the boss HP increases to 150,000, culminating in a final showdown at Wave 70 where the boss has up to 360,000 HP. Clearing Wave 70 guarantees a chance at rare accessories and character traits.

To tackle these scaling health pools, you must choose a build that maximizes your damage output while keeping you safe from area-of-effect attacks.

<Section icon={Target} title="High-Tier Raid Progression and Stat Requirements">

Once you have mastered the entry-level content, the step up to high-tier raids requires a complete overhaul of your gear and stat distribution. In these advanced stages, even basic trash mobs have millions of health points, making standard physical attacks virtually useless. You will need to rely heavily on maxed-out skills and specialized builds.

In mid-tier raids, enemies at Wave 50 regularly boast over 2.5 million HP. By the time you reach the final waves of the most difficult raids, the health pools reach astronomical numbers, peaking at a massive 15 billion HP. To deal with these absurd numbers, players must utilize high-damage skills like Dismantle and Malevolent Shrine, which can deal millions of damage per second when paired with maxed-out offensive stats.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Optimize Base Stats">
    Farm the Culling Games raid repeatedly until you have enough stat points to allocate at least 30k to 50k points into your primary damage attribute.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Acquire AoE Skills">
    Unlock high-range AoE techniques. Moves like Dismantle are essential because they allow you to damage multiple high-HP enemies simultaneously.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Master Enemy Luring">
    Avoid running into the center of the arena. Lure enemies to the edges or corners to group them tightly, making your AoE skills highly efficient.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Burst Rotations">
    Chain your crowd control skills with your ultimate damage moves to melt bosses before they can trigger their high-damage phase transitions.
  </Step>
</Steps>
