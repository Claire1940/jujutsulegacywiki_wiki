<Section icon={Zap} title="Wave Progression and Boss Mechanics">

Success in the **jujutsu legacy divine general raid** depends on understanding the specific rotation of enemies. The raid follows a strict cycle where the mobs and bosses change every five waves. This cycle is critical for players farming the **Demonic Usurpation trait**, as the drop chance is tied to the completion of these specific milestones.

The progression begins with Shibuya Sukuna, who focuses on high-speed melee and fire-based area-of-effect attacks. As you clear wave 5, the raid transitions to Meguna, who introduces the summoning mechanics you will later use yourself. Following Meguna is Ajito, a beast-like summon known for its speed and pressure, and finally, the Divine General Mahoraga himself appears.

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
Mahoraga in this raid features an adaptation mechanic. If the fight lasts too long, his resistance to your primary Cursed Technique will increase, significantly reducing your damage output. Switch between M1s and skills to keep him off-balance.
</Callout>

| Wave Range | Boss Encounter | Primary Threat | Strategy |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **1 - 5** | Shibuya Sukuna | Fire Arrow & Cleave | Maintain distance/Dodge |
| **6 - 10** | Meguna | Summons (Ajito/Mahoraga) | Focus on the summoner |
| **11 - 15** | Ajito | High Mobility Strikes | Use stuns or barriers |
| **16 - 20** | Mahoraga | Adaptation & Heavy Cleave | Burst damage/Cycle skills |
| **21+** | Cycle Repeats | Increased HP/Damage | Resource management |

Once Mahoraga is defeated at wave 20, the cycle restarts with a buffed version of Shibuya Sukuna. Most players aim to reach at least wave 20 to maximize their chances of seeing the rare trait drop notification.

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Evolve: The Demonic Usurpation Process">

Acquiring the Awakened version of Ten Shadows is a multi-step process that requires both luck and perseverance. The **jujutsu legacy divine general raid** is the only place where the final piece of the puzzle can be found. You do not necessarily need to be using Ten Shadows to farm the raid, but you must have the base technique to perform the final evolution.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtain Base 10 Shadows">
    You must first roll for the standard Ten Shadows Cursed Technique. This has a base pull rate of **0.1%**, making it one of the rarest base techniques in the game.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Enter the Culling Games Raid">
    Travel to the Culling Games area and locate the raid entrance. You can enter solo or with a team. The raid is endless, so prepare for a long session.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Farm for Demonic Usurpation">
    Battle through the waves. You are looking for the **Demonic Usurpation trait**, which has a **0.2% drop rate**. This usually appears as a prompt on your screen after defeating a boss wave.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Activate and Exit">
    Once the trait drops, you must **activate it immediately** within the raid menu. After activation, you can safely exit the raid through the designated portal or by completing the current wave.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Final Awakening">
    Upon exiting with the active trait and the base 10 Shadows equipped, your technique will automatically evolve into **10 Shadows Awakened (Meguna)**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Trait Rarity">
Note that the 0.2% drop rate is per boss cycle. While difficult to obtain, the power jump from standard 10 Shadows to Meguna is considered the largest in the current 2026 meta.
</Callout>

</Section>
