<Section icon={Target} title="Leveling Route and Boss Drops">

To reach the level cap of 2000 efficiently, you must follow a structured leveling route. Wandering into high-level zones prematurely will result in rapid defeats and slowed progression. Use the quest system to guide your geographic movement across the map.

### Recommended Leveling Milestones

1. **Levels 1 to 125**: Focus entirely on Jujutsu High. Complete starter quests and farm basic curses.
2. **Levels 125 to 275**: Move toward the Shibuya Approach and complete the Eso quest chain.
3. **Levels 300 to 450**: Enter the Shibuya Metro. Farm Choso and Mahito for consistent experience.
4. **Levels 500 to 700**: Progress through the Culling Games. Target JudgeMan, Naoya, and Higuruma.
5. **Levels 1000 to 2000**: Enter Shinjuku and prepare for high-level raids.
