### Sukuna Binding Vow Combat Overview

The Sukuna Binding Vow boss utilizes rapid slicing attacks and area-of-effect fire spells. Players must maintain mobility to avoid the Cleave and Dismantle abilities. Bringing a healer or utilizing high-regeneration builds can help mitigate the constant passive damage during the fight.

Ranged builds generally perform better here, as they allow players to stay out of the immediate range of the boss's physical strikes. If you are playing a melee build, timing your parries and dodges is critical to surviving the aggressive attack phases.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Unlocking the Sukuna Vessel Movesets">

Consuming **jujutsu legacy sukuna fingers** grants players access to some of the most destructive movesets in the game. These movesets alter your combat style, replacing standard attacks with high-damage slashing techniques and domain expansions.

There are two primary tiers of unlocks associated with these fingers. The first tier grants the basic Sukuna Vessel moveset, which offers strong offensive pressure. The second tier is the Heian Era Sukuna Vessel moveset, which is widely considered an endgame powerhouse.

| Moveset Tier | Finger Requirement | Key Abilities | Playstyle Focus |
| **Sukuna Vessel** | 1 to 19 Fingers | Cleave, Dismantle, Fire Arrow | High burst damage, medium range |
| **Heian Era Sukuna Vessel** | 20 Fingers | Malevolent Shrine, Enhanced Cleave | Area denial, massive DPS, crowd control |

To unlock the Heian Era variant, you must consume exactly 20 fingers. Consuming fewer than this amount will only grant partial power or the standard moveset. This makes the grind to 20 fingers a major milestone for players seeking to dominate PvP and PvE activities.

<Callout type="tip" title="Progression Tip">

</Callout>

### Heian Era Capabilities

Once you reach the 20-finger threshold, your character gains access to the Malevolent Shrine domain expansion. This ability deals continuous slashing damage to all enemies within a massive radius. It bypasses standard defenses, making it highly effective against bosses and grouping enemies in raids.

Additionally, the Heian Era moveset enhances your physical attributes, increasing your base health pool and speed. This allows you to sustain longer fights without relying heavily on defensive support items.
