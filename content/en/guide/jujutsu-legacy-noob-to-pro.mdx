<Section icon={Target} title="Phase 4: The Leveling Route to 2000">

The journey to the Level 2000 cap is segmented into four major geographic zones. Efficiency depends on following quest chains rather than farming random mobs. Pro players use a "Quest-Boss Loop" where they accept a repeatable quest, defeat the target boss, and turn in the quest simultaneously for double rewards.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Jujutsu High (Levels 1-125)">
    Complete the initial training quests and focus on Takuma Ino. This area is designed to familiarize you with the M-Menu and basic combat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Shibuya Metro (Levels 300-500)">
    This is the first major wall. You must farm Choso and Mahito repeatedly. Ensure your technique is at least A-Tier before attempting these bosses.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Culling Games (Levels 500-1000)">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Shinjuku Raids (Levels 1000-2000)">
    The final stretch involves the Strongest Raid and Divine General Raid. You cannot solo these effectively without a complete Vessel-Technique-Accessory setup.
  </Step>
</Steps>
