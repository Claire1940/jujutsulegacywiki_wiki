### Questing Basics & Rules

Before embarking on your questing journey, you must familiarize yourself with the fundamental rules of the game's quest system. You cannot progress efficiently if you do not understand how quests interact with your character's stats and techniques.

| Mechanic | Detail | Requirement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Quest Limit** | Only **1 active quest** can be tracked at any given time. | None |
| **Cancelation** | Quests can be canceled instantly and for **free** via the menu. | None |
| **Rewards** | All completed quests award both **Yen** and character **EXP**. | Quest Completion |
| **Code Unlock** | Code redemption is locked until you reach **Level 5**. | Reach Level 5 |
| **Simple Domain** | Defensive barrier skill used to counter domain expansions. | Level 250 + 100k Yen |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip: Cursed Energy Upgrades">
The very first thing you should purchase after securing a decent cursed technique is a **Cursed Energy upgrade**. This upgrade significantly boosts your technique damage, allowing you to defeat quest mobs much faster.
</Callout>

### Step-by-Step Starter Leveling Route

Follow this optimized path to navigate the first 300 levels of your sorcerer's career without getting stuck on difficult bosses.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Grind Students to Level 5">
    Spawn in and immediately take the Level 1 quest to defeat Jujutsu Students. Put all of your early stat points into **Melee** to maximize your basic combat damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Redeem Active Codes">
    As soon as you hit Level 5, open your menu (M key) and redeem all active codes. Use your free Technique Spins to roll for a strong farming technique like **Disaster Flames** or **Ten Shadows**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Farm Fly Heads and Slimes">
    Transition to the Level 10 Fly Heads quest, then the Level 25 Slime Curses quest. Once you have a decent technique, purchase a **Cursed Energy upgrade** to scale your skill damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Conquer Shibuya Checkpoints">
    Move to Shibuya at Level 125. Farm the Level 150 Street Slimes until you are strong enough to defeat the Level 200 Hanami and Level 250 Finger Bearer bosses.
  </Step>
</Steps>
