When you first spawn into the game, you will find yourself at the Jujutsu School. This starting zone is designed to carry you from Level 1 to Level 99. The immediate area around the main school building contains essential service NPCs, including the spin dealers for races, clans, and cursed techniques. Before rushing out into combat, redeem any active promo codes to secure technique spins. Highly efficient farming techniques like Disaster Flames (which has a 25% roll chance) are exceptional for early progression because they allow you to deal massive area-of-effect damage.

The early mobs in this area hit surprisingly hard. Therefore, setting your spawn point in the forest is a highly recommended strategy to minimize downtime when you inevitably fall in battle. Focus all your early stat points into melee damage until you secure a reliable cursed technique. Once you have a decent technique, purchase upgrades for your cursed energy to scale your damage output.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Shibuya Station & Metro Leveling Routes">

Shibuya Station is the core mid-game hub on the **jujutsu legacy map**, designed for players between Level 100 and 499. Once you reach Level 100, speak with the Yuji NPC at the Jujutsu School spawn to teleport directly to the city of Shibuya. 

Inside Shibuya, the gameplay dynamic shifts from simple field mobs to challenging boss encounters and complex underground metro networks. The Shibuya Metro is an exceptional zone for farming both Yen and Experience. For instance, the Level 300 quest inside the metro is highly regarded as the absolute best spot to accumulate money quickly, especially if you utilize high-damage, wide-range techniques like Satoru Gojo's Red or Suguru Geto's Curse Manipulation.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Culling Games & High-Level Raids">

After conquering Shibuya, players must venture into the Culling Games area, which serves as the late-game progression zone spanning Levels 500 to 900. The entrance to this zone is situated right next to the Shibuya Metro Teleporter. 

The Culling Games area introduces highly resilient opponents and bosses that require optimized builds and high-rarity clans, such as the Ryomen clan. Quests in this region scale in increments of 50 levels, leading players along a winding road filled with iconic characters. 

In addition to standard quest grinding, the Culling Games area is home to the Strongest Raid and the Divine General Raid. Raids are wave-based challenges where players can clear up to 1000 waves of increasingly difficult enemies. These raids are the premier method for obtaining rare titles, fighting styles, traits, vessels, and S-tier accessories.

| Quest Giver / Boss | Level Requirement | Map Location Details | Key Drops / Rewards |
| Panda | Level 500 | Left at Culling Games spawn | Progression XP |
| Reincarnated Sorcerer | Level 650 | Follow main road straight | 1% Ishigori Hair |
| Kinji Hakari | Level 700 | Past Reincarnated Sorcerer | High Yen Payout |
| Maki Zen'in | Level 750 | Follow winding path | Progression XP |
| Yorozu | Level 800 | Near end of the road | Progression XP |
| Meguna | Level 900 | Final area of Culling Games | 0.1% Meguna Haori |

<Callout type="success" title="Meguna & Ten Shadows V2">
The Culling Games area hosts the Meguna raid. Acquiring the rare Demonic Usurpation trait (0.2% drop chance) unlocks the devastating Ten Shadows Awakened (V2) technique, which features powerful summons like Agito and Mahoraga that execute coordinated attacks alongside your basic strikes.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Shinjuku End-Game Bosses & Vessel Items">

Shinjuku, also known as the Shinjuku Collapse zone, represents the absolute pinnacle of end-game content on the **jujutsu legacy map**. This area is designed for max-level players looking to optimize their gear, obtain legendary cosmetics, and unlock the most powerful vessels in the game.

Shinjuku features legendary encounters such as Satoru Gojo V2, True Heian Sukuna, and Kashimo. Fighting these bosses is highly demanding, but the rewards are unmatched. For example, defeating Gojo V2 offers a chance to drop the Gojo Haori, which doubles your health and exponentially boosts your Infinity technique damage.

| Boss Name | Spawn Location | Required Item / Quest | Drop Rates & Items |
| Gojo V2 | Shinjuku Center | Max Level Recommended | 20% Gojo Haori |
| True Heian Sukuna | Big green tree area | Divergent Fist recommended | Sukuna Fingers |
| Kashimo | Behind Uraume spawn | Defeat repeatedly | Kashimo Head (Vessel) |
| Divine General | Right side of city entrance | Level 5000+ Quest | Raid Trigger Orb |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtain Base Sukuna Vessel">
    Collect 2 to 4 Sukuna Fingers by defeating Sukuna Binding Vow or Sukuna Prime in Shinjuku.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Reach Shibuya Level Requirements">
    Progress to Level 475 and locate the Sukuna boss spawn point in Shibuya Station.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Amass 20 Sukuna Fingers">
