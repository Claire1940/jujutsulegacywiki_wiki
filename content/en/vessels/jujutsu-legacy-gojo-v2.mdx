<Section icon={Swords} title="Boss Fight Mechanics and Moveset">

Defeating the boss requires a deep understanding of his high-damage moveset. He utilizes advanced spatial manipulation techniques that can quickly deplete your health bar if you fail to dodge or block at the correct times. His attacks are fast, cover a wide area, and often bypass standard defensive guards.

His primary offensive rotation consists of four signature moves. Understanding the visual cues for each attack is the key to surviving the encounter.

|Move Name|Damage Type|Visual Cue|Counter Strategy|
|---|---|---|---|
|**Lapse Blue**|Vacuum/Pull|Blue glowing aura around hands|Dash backward immediately to avoid the pull radius.|
|**Reversal Red**|Explosive Knockback|Red spark in front of the boss|Hold block to reduce damage and prevent knockback.|
|**Hollow Purple**|Linear Heavy Projectile|Large purple sphere charging|Dash laterally to the left or right of the projectile path.|
|**Unlimited Void**|AOE Domain Stun|Dark barrier expanding|Run out of the expanding dome before the barrier closes.|

<Callout type="warning" title="Danger Alert">

</Callout>
