<Section icon={Target} title="A-Tier and B-Tier: Specialized Utility">

Not every player needs to chase the S-tier meta to be successful. A-tier vessels like **Uro** and **Ishigori** offer specialized tools that excel in specific environments, such as ranged spacing or boss farming.

**Uro** is the queen of spacing. By utilizing the **Sky Manipulation** technique, Uro users can stay safe from most ground-based attacks while dealing consistent damage. This makes Uro an excellent choice for players who prefer a "safe" playstyle during difficult boss encounters like **JudgeMan** or **Toji Reincarnation**.

**Ishigori** focuses on raw, direct ranged pressure. His signature **Cursed Energy Discharge** allows for heavy burst windows. While he lacks the mobility of Kashimo or the raw power of Sukuna, his consistency in landing hits from a distance makes him a top-tier choice for farming the Metro bosses.

| Vessel | Area | Farming Target | Notable Reward |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Uro** | Culling Games | Uro Boss | Sky Fragment |
| **Ishigori** | Culling Games | Ishigori Boss | Ishigori Hair |
| **Mechamaru** | Jujutsu High | Special Quests | Puppet Components |

**Mechamaru** sits in the B-tier, primarily because his value is strictly tied to the **Puppet Manipulation** technique. While powerful in the right hands, it requires a very specific build and investment into mechanical pressure that many players find too niche for general progression.

<Callout type="warning" title="Build Warning">
Do not switch to Mechamaru unless you have already committed to a full Puppet Manipulation build. Without the specific technique synergy, this vessel performs significantly worse than even base-level sorcerers.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Obtain and Upgrade Your Vessel">

Obtaining a vessel in Jujutsu Legacy is a multi-step process that involves reaching specific level milestones and defeating powerful bosses. Unlike techniques, which are primarily obtained through spins, vessels often require tangible items dropped from the world's most dangerous enemies.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Level 500+">
    Most vessel-related bosses are located in the Culling Games or Shinjuku areas. You should be at least level 500 to survive these encounters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate the Boss">
    Travel to the specific area for your desired vessel. For example, Uro and Ishigori are found in the Culling Games region.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Farm the Vessel Item">
    Defeat the boss repeatedly to obtain their unique drop (e.g., Sky Fragment for Uro or Kashimo Head for Kashimo). Drop rates vary, so patience is key.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Complete the Ritual/Quest">
    Some vessels require an additional quest or NPC interaction once you have the item. Check the official Trello for specific ritual locations.
  </Step>
</Steps>
