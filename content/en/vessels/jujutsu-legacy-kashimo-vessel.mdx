<Section icon={Map} title="Acquisition Path: How to Obtain the Kashimo Vessel">

Unlocking the **jujutsu legacy kashimo vessel** is a multi-step process that requires reaching the endgame areas. You cannot simply roll for this vessel; you must prove your strength by defeating the God of Lightning himself in the Shinjuku district.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Level 1000+">
    Ensure your character is at least Level 1000. While the level cap is 2000, attempting the Shinjuku route earlier will result in frequent deaths due to the high damage output of late-game NPCs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate to Shinjuku">
    Move past the Culling Games area and enter the Shinjuku route. This is the current highest-level zone in the game where the most powerful bosses reside.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Farm the Kashimo Boss">
    Locate the Kashimo boss. He is known for his aggressive lightning rushes. You will need to defeat him repeatedly to obtain the **Kashimo Head** item, which has a low drop rate.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Interact with the Vessel NPC">
    Once you have the Kashimo Head, take it to the designated Vessel NPC to consume the item and bind the Kashimo vessel to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Equip and Level Up">
    Equip the vessel in your menu. You will now need to level up your vessel mastery to unlock the full potential of its passive lightning buffs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Preparation Required">
The Kashimo boss fight is mechanically intense. He uses high-speed dashes that can break your guard. It is highly recommended to bring a friend or use a technique with high stun potential like "Ice Formation" to keep him in place.
</Callout>
