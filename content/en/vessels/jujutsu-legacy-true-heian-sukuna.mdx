Reaching the final form of the King of Curses requires a precise combination of items, traits, and NPC interactions. Below is the step-by-step progression path you must follow to transform your character into the True Heian Sukuna vessel.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Collect and Consume 20 Sukuna Fingers">
    Travel to Shinjuku and locate the Sukuna Binding Vow boss. You can find him by following the main road straight until you reach the massive green tree with two branches, then looking to your right. Defeat him repeatedly to collect 20 fingers. Consume all 20 fingers to unlock the base Heian Sukuna vessel moveset.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip the Heian Sukuna Vessel">
    Visit the Metro area and speak to the Vessel Switcher NPC. Swap your active vessel to Heian Sukuna. If you have the Ten Shadows V2 technique and the Demonic Usurpation trait, you can also use the Switch Transformation skill to swap freely.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Obtain and Equip the Ancient Malevolence Trait">
    Reroll your traits using spins or code rewards until you secure the Ancient Malevolence Trait. This specific trait is a mandatory requirement to trigger the final evolution of the vessel.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Finalize the True Heian Sukuna Evolution">
    With the Heian Sukuna vessel active, the Ancient Malevolence Trait equipped, and 20 fingers consumed, your character will permanently transition to the True Heian Sukuna state, unlocking the ultimate moveset.
  </Step>
</Steps>

It is important to manage your spin resources wisely during this process. Trait spins can be rare, so saving your codes and spin rewards for a dedicated reroll session is the most efficient way to secure the Ancient Malevolence Trait without stalling your progression.
