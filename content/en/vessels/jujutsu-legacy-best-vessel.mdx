Securing your desired vessel requires a combination of progression, boss farming, and luck with spins. Because the game utilizes a gacha-style reroll system for techniques, clans, and races, you must be strategic with your resources to avoid wasting the free rewards provided by the developers.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Level 5">
    Before you can even think about rerolling for the **jujutsu legacy best vessel**, you must reach Level 5. This unlocks the ability to use the in-game menu (M key) and the code redemption box.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Claim Free Spins">
    Use active codes such as **MutualLove** or **Mahoraga** to stock up on Technique and Clan Spins. These are your primary currency for changing your character's core identity.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Farm Boss-Specific Items">
    Certain vessels require specific drops. For example, to fully realize an **Ishigori** build, you should farm the **Ishigori Hair** in the Culling Games area.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute the Reroll Plan">
    Do not reroll one spin at a time. Save a batch of 20-30 spins and only commit to a reroll once you have a backup plan or enough spins to reach a usable tier.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Finalize with Raids">
    Once your vessel and technique are set, enter the **Divine General Raid** to farm for late-game modifiers like **Demonic Usurpation** or **Heian Emperor**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
