<Section icon={Map} title="The Zenin Boss Route & Farming Strategy">

Progression as a Zenin member involves a specific "Zenin route" within the **Culling Games** and surrounding areas. This route is essential for obtaining the best swords and accessories that complement the clan's high-speed playstyle.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Shibuya Bridge Checkpoint">
    Locate **Megumi** near the Shibuya bridge route. He is a mid-game boss (Level 125-275+) who drops the **Dark Surge** sword and the **Shadow Title**. This is the first major milestone for any Zenin build.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Jujutsu High Side Quest">
    Seek out the **Toji Reincarnation** boss. This fight is located on a side route near Jujutsu High. Defeating him is the only way to farm for the **Playful Cloud** and the legendary **Inverted Spear of Heaven**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Culling Games Entry">
    Once you reach Level 500+, enter the Culling Games. Follow the **Zenin route** to find **Naoya**. This boss is a test of your mobility and reaction time, but the rewards are vital for late-game scaling.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The JudgeMan Encounter">
    On the same route, you will encounter **JudgeMan**. While not strictly a Zenin, his drops—the **Gavel Hammer** and **Executioner Sword**—are essential weapon upgrades for sword-focused Zenin builds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Toji Boss Difficulty">
The **Toji Reincarnation** boss is notorious for his high speed and lack of cursed energy, making traditional "stun-lock" techniques less effective. It is recommended to fight him with at least one teammate or a high-level **Ten Shadows** summon to draw aggro.
</Callout>

Understanding how to manage your **Clan Spins** is what separates a novice from a pro in Jujutsu Legacy. Since the **jujutsu legacy zenin clan** is a high-demand roll, you must use your resources wisely.

The game requires you to reach **Level 5** before you can redeem codes for free spins. Once you have a bank of spins, follow this priority list:

1.  **Technique First**: Never roll your clan until you have a technique you are happy with. A Zenin clan with a "Divergent Fist" technique is a wasted build.
2.  **Vessel Synergy**: If you are aiming for the **Sukuna** or **Ishigori** vessel, ensure your clan passives don't conflict with your vessel's stat requirements.
3.  **The "Stop" Rule**: If you hit Zenin, Gojo, or Kamo, **stop spinning**. These are the "Big Three" and will carry you to the Level 2000 cap easily.
