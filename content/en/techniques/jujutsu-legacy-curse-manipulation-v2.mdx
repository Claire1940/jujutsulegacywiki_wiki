<Section icon={Target} title="Unlock Walkthrough: Step-by-Step V2 Requirements">

To successfully awaken **jujutsu legacy curse manipulation v2**, you must assemble three distinct components and equip them at the same time. Failing to have even one of these equipped will prevent the V2 moveset from activating, leaving you with the standard V1 skills. 

The three required components are: the base Curse Manipulation technique, the Spirit Infiltrator trait, and the Kenjaku Vessel. Obtaining the Kenjaku Vessel is the most time-consuming step, as it requires you to battle high-HP bosses in Shinjuku to obtain extremely rare drops.

| Required Component | Obtaining Method | Drop / Obtain Rate |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Curse Manipulation** | Spin Technique NPC or Perm Seller | 0.1% Spin Chance / 500 Robux |
| **Spirit Infiltrator Trait** | Shinjuku Boss Drop / Trait Spins | Rare Drop / Spin |
| **Kenjaku Vessel** | Metro Vessel Switcher NPC | Swapped using Brain & Brain Implant |
| **Brain (Vessel Item)** | Golden Era Raid (Shinjuku) | 1.0% Drop Rate |
| **Brain Implant (Vessel Item)** | Golden Era Raid (Shinjuku) | 0.5% Drop Rate |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire the Base Technique">
    Obtain the base Curse Manipulation technique. You can roll for this at Jujutsu High using your accumulated Technique Spins (0.1% chance) or purchase it directly from the Permanent Technique Seller.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm the Shinjuku Raid">
    Travel to Shinjuku and locate the Golden Era Raid next to the Mahoraga boss arena. Defeat the raid bosses repeatedly to obtain the Brain (1% drop rate) and the Brain Implant (0.5% drop rate).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Acquire the Spirit Infiltrator Trait">
    Equip the Spirit Infiltrator trait on your character. This trait is required to bridge the connection between your curse-swallowing technique and the Kenjaku physical form.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Speak to the Vessel Switcher NPC">
    Head into the Metro and locate the Vessel Switcher NPC. Talk to him to transition your active vessel to Kenjaku. Ensure you have progressed through previous vessel tiers (Sukuna, Kashimo, Ishigori) as required.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Equip All Three to Awaken V2">
    Open your character menu and verify that you have the Curse Manipulation technique, Kenjaku Vessel, and Spirit Infiltrator trait active. Your moveset will automatically transform into the V2 variant.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Vessel Warning">
The Kenjaku Vessel does absolutely nothing on its own. If you swap into it without having both the base Curse Manipulation technique and the Spirit Infiltrator trait equipped, your character will have no special moveset and will default to basic combat. Always ensure all three parts of the build are active.
