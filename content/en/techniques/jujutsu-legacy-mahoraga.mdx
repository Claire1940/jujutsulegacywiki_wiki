<Section icon={Swords} title="How to Defeat and Tame Mahoraga">

The fight to tame the **jujutsu legacy mahoraga** boss is one of the most challenging solo encounters in the game. If another player assists you during the fight, the taming process invalidates, and you must start over. The boss utilizes a unique adaptation mechanic. Every time you strike the boss with a specific damage type, the wheel on its head turns. After eight turns of the wheel, the boss becomes immune to that damage type for the remainder of the fight.

To defeat the boss, you must cycle your movesets constantly. Do not spam the same skill sequence. Use physical strikes, fire-based curse attacks, and weapon skills in a rotating sequence.

| Phase | HP Range | Active Boss Skills | Counter Strategy |
|--- |--- |--- |--- |
| Phase 1 | 100% to 75% | Basic sword strikes | Perfect block and counter |
| Phase 2 | 74% to 40% | Ground slams, wheel turns | Cycle damage types rapidly |
| Phase 3 | 39% to 0% | Sword of Extermination | Keep distance, use ranged skills |

<Callout type="warning" title="Adaptation Alert">
If you see the wheel glow gold, stop using your current skill immediately. This visual cue indicates the boss has adapted to your active element, reducing your damage to zero.
</Callout>

Follow these precise steps during the encounter to secure your victory:
