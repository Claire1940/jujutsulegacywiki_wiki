The **jujutsu legacy puppet manipulation** technique is inspired by the character Kokichi Muta from the Jujutsu Kaisen series. In this game, it serves as a high-tier Cursed Technique that focuses on mechanical prowess and long-range energy projection. Unlike some techniques that are granted through spins, this specific power is tied to boss progression and item drops, making it a badge of honor for dedicated players.

To begin your journey as a puppet master, you must challenge Mechamaru. This boss encounter is the primary source for the technique's base form. The item required to unlock the power is the *Puppet Manipulation* item itself.

<Section icon={Target} title="Evolution Guide: Unlocking V2">

Upgrading to **jujutsu legacy puppet manipulation V2** is a multi-step process that requires a specific character build. You cannot simply "find" V2; you must evolve into it by aligning your Vessel, Trait, and Technique. This transformation represents the "Ultimate Mechamaru" form, significantly increasing your offensive and defensive stats.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Equip the Mechamaru Vessel">
    You must have the Mechamaru Vessel active. This vessel provides the necessary biological/mechanical compatibility for the advanced puppet techniques.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Roll for Heavenly Core Trait">
    The Heavenly Core Trait is mandatory. This represents the "Heavenly Restriction" that grants the user immense cursed energy and range at the cost of physical health or mobility in lore, though in-game it acts as a power multiplier.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Master the Base Technique">
    Ensure the Puppet Manipulation technique is currently equipped in your primary slot. You cannot evolve the technique if it is stored in your inventory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Activate Mode: Absolute">
    Once all requirements are met, you gain access to the "Ultimate Mechamaru - Mode: Absolute" skill. Activating this triggers the transformation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Requirement Check">
If you are missing even one of these three components—the Vessel, the Trait, or the Technique—the V2 skills will not trigger. Most players struggle with the Heavenly Core Trait, as it often requires multiple rerolls to obtain.
</Callout>

The V2 transformation lasts for 30 seconds initially. However, there is a unique mechanic where returning to human mode after the transformation allows you to retain the upgraded moveset while gaining a massive defensive buff.

</Section>
