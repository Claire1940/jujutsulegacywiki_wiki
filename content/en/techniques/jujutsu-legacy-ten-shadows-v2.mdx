Navigating the world of Jujutsu Legacy requires a structured approach to questing. The current Level Cap of 2000 means you cannot simply grind the same area indefinitely. You must move through specific "progression bands" to keep your experience gain optimal.

  <Step num="1" title="The Starter Phase (Levels 1-125)">

  <Step num="2" title="Shibuya Metro (Levels 300-450)">

  <Step num="4" title="Endgame Shinjuku (Levels 1000-2000)">
    The final stretch involves raiding and fighting Sukuna-level bosses. At this stage, your build must be fully optimized with S-tier techniques and legendary accessories.
  </Step>
</Steps>
